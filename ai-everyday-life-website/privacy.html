Privacy Policy

How we protect your privacy and handle your information

Last Updated: June 30, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Everyday AI's Privacy Policy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of any personal information you provide.

Information We Don't Collect

As an educational website focused on providing information about artificial intelligence, we prioritize user privacy. We do not:

  • Use cookies for tracking purposes
  • Collect personal information without explicit consent
  • Share any user information with third parties
  • Store user browsing history
  • Use any form of behavioral tracking

Newsletter Subscription

If you choose to subscribe to our newsletter, we collect:

  • Your email address
  • Subscription preferences

This information is used solely for:

  • Sending our newsletter
  • Providing updates about AI technology
  • Sharing educational content

You can unsubscribe at any time using the link provided in each newsletter.

Contact Form

When you use our contact form, we collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Message content

This information is used only to respond to your inquiries and is not stored longer than necessary.

Third-Party Content

Our website includes:

  • Images from various sources, properly credited
  • Links to external websites
  • Embedded content (like maps or videos)

We ensure all third-party content is used in compliance with relevant licenses and permissions.

Data Security

We protect your information through:

  • Secure HTTPS connections
  • Regular security updates
  • Limited access to user data
  • Secure data storage practices

Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Withdraw consent for data collection
  • Receive a copy of your data
